**_7. Our manufacturing facilities are under-utilized and the capacity utilisation of our primary manufacturing facilities further  declined in Fiscal 2023 as compared to Fiscal 2022 and 2021. Under-utilisation of our existing or future primary manufacturing facilities in the future may have an adverse impact on our business, results of operations, financial condition and cash flows._** 

Our manufacturing facilities are currently under-utilised and the capacity utilisation of our primary manufacturing facilities further declined from 40.08% in Fiscal 2021 to 32.36% in Fiscal 2022 and further to 27.56% in Fiscal 2023. The capacity utilization is affected by our product mix, our ability to accurately forecast customer demand, to carry out uninterrupted operations, the availability of raw materials, and industry/ market conditions. In the event there is a decline in the demand for our products, or if we face prolonged disruptions at our facilities or are unable to procure sufficient raw materials, our capacity utilisation would further decline and we would not be able to achieve full capacity utilization of our existing or future manufacturing facilities, resulting in operational inefficiencies which could have an adverse effect on our business, results of operations, financial condition and cash flows. Our capacity utilization of the primary manufacturing facilities which focus on the manufacturing of our finished products in Fiscal 2023 declined as compared to the capacity utilization of the manufacturing facilities in Fiscal 2022 and 2021 due to following reasons: 

- In the case of the primary facilities located at Rajkot, Gujarat and Nagpur, Maharashtra, we reduced the grammage per pouch offered in our ₹ 5 and ₹ 10 SKUs in the third and fourth quarters of Fiscal 2021 and during the first quarter of Fiscal 2023 which resulted in a decrease in actual production in Fiscal 2022 as

compared to Fiscal 2021 and decrease in actual production in Fiscal 2023 as compared to Fiscal 2022, leading to a decrease in capacity utilisation. Further, in the case of the primary facility located at Nagpur, Maharashtra, the production declined in Fiscal 2023 as compared to Fiscal 2022 due to a decline in sales. 

- In the case of the primary facility located at Nagpur, Maharashtra, the available capacity increased in Fiscal 2022 as compared to Fiscal 2021, while the actual production reduced during Fiscal 2022 as compared to Fiscal 2021 due to the reasons cited in above, which resulted in a decrease in capacity utilization in Fiscal 2022 as compared to Fiscal 2021. 

- In the case of the primary facility located at Modasa, Gujarat, the available capacity increased by 176.64% from 7,615.67 MT in Fiscal 2022 to 21,068.11 MT in Fiscal 2023 due to capacity expansion during Fiscal 2023. **_10. Our business is subject to seasonality. Lower revenues outside of the festive period and when the schools are on summer break may adversely affect our business, results of operations, financial condition and cash flows._**

Further, we observe a decline in the sale of products during the summer season, especially when schools are on summer break.
