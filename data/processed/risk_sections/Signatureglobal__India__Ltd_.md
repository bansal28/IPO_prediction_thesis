Factors related to the COVID-19 pandemic, or a future pandemic, that have had or could in the future have an impact on our business operations and financial performance, include: 

- A complete or partial closure of, or other operational issues at, one or more of our construction sites, resulting from government action; while operations at all of our construction sites were suspended due to the government directives, we were able to resume operations sites with certain restrictions once the restrictions were lifted; and 

- The potential negative effect on the health of our personnel, particularly if a significant number of them are, affected by COVID-19, could impact our ability to ensure business continuity during this disruption. 

Initially, the COVID-19 pandemic resulted in construction delays at our project sites due to several factors such as lockdowns enforced by the government agencies, work-stoppage orders, disruptions in the supply of materials and shortage of labour.
